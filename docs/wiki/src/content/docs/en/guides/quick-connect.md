description: Quickly access your hosts using Quick Connect.
---

Remora provides quick connect functionality to help you quickly access your frequently used SSH hosts.

## Quick Connect

Select a host from the sidebar, double-click or press Enter to connect.

## Search Hosts

Enter hostname or IP in the search box to filter hosts.

## New Connection

1. Click the **+** button in the sidebar
2. Enter host address, port, and username
3. Choose authentication method (password or SSH key)
4. Click **Connect**
